Hi, I'm Astaroth

Hello, you can call me Chris, and this is my journey. I enjoy to work in 3D since it sets creativity free and may even end up as art which might inspire others. I've been using Blender for one year and I'm pretty confident in my skills, though, there's still place to learn as learning never stops but I'm ready for any challenge you might come up with.

My commissions usually don't take too long and I take in mind to model with as less triangles as possible, clean normals and uv maps.
